# Mobile Responsiveness Assessment & Fixes

## Current Status

### ✅ What Works Well
- Responsive grid layouts (`md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3`)
- Responsive typography (`text-5xl md:text-6xl`)
- Padding adjustments (`px-4 sm:px-6 lg:px-8`)
- Cards stack on mobile

### ❌ Issues Found

1. **Navbar** - No mobile menu (links hidden on mobile)
2. **Touch Targets** - Some buttons/icons may be too small
3. **WorkoutEditor** - Long forms may be cramped
4. **Button Text** - May overflow on small screens
5. **Image Upload** - Large padding on mobile
